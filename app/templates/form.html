How to Submit a Model or Request Help

  • For information on how to contribute your Cascadia Earth model to CRESCENT, visit our Model Contribution Guidelines page.

  • To ask questions regarding CVM web/tools, follow the steps outlined below:
    1. Go to the CRESCENT-CVM GitHub Issue Page — Click here to be taken directly to our GitHub issue tracker.
    2. Create a New Issue — Once on the page, click on the "New Issue" button to create a new submission or help request.
    3. Provide Details —
      • Include a brief description of your question or issue.
      • Attach or link any relevant files, if applicable.
    4. Submit — After providing the necessary details, submit the issue. Our team will review your request and respond as soon as possible.

We look forward to your submissions and are happy to assist with any questions!
